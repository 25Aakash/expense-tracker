### 2. Financial Information

- **Category Information:** Expense and income categories you use or create
- **Reports and Analytics:** Aggregated financial data and statistics you generate

### 3. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, screens viewed, time spent in the app
- **Performance Data:** App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
- **Network Information:** IP address, connection type

### 4. Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect actual bank account numbers or financial institution credentials
- We do NOT access your device's camera or photo library
- We do NOT track your physical location via GPS
- We do NOT collect biometric data
